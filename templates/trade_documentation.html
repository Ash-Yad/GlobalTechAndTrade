Essential Trade Documents

We prepare and verify all documents required for smooth international trade.

Commercial Invoice

The primary document for customs valuation and duty calculation.

  • Buyer/seller details
  • Product description
  • Value & Incoterms

Packing List

Detailed breakdown of shipment contents for customs and logistics.

  • Package details
  • Weights & dimensions
  • Marking & numbering

Bill of Lading

Contract of carriage and document of title for sea freight.

  • Original B/L
  • Telex release
  • Sea waybill

Certificate of Origin

Certifies where goods were manufactured for preferential duties.

  • Non-preferential CO
  • Preferential CO (FTA)
  • GSP certificates

Phytosanitary Certificate

Required for plant products to certify pest-free status.

  • Agricultural exports
  • Plant products
  • Seeds & grains

Inspection Certificates

Third-party verification of quality, quantity, and specifications.

  • Pre-shipment inspection
  • Quality certificates
  • Weight certificates

Health Certificates

Required for food products and animal-origin goods.

  • Veterinary certificates
  • Food safety certs
  • Halal certificates

Insurance Certificate

Proof of cargo insurance coverage for LC compliance.

  • Marine insurance
  • Air cargo insurance
  • All-risk coverage

Export Licenses

Government permits for controlled or restricted goods.

  • DGFT licenses
  • Dual-use permits
  • Special permits
