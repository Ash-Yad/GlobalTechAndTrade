Google Business Profile

Appear in Google Maps and local search results. Essential for local visibility.

Action Required: Set up profiles for India and Zambia offices

How to set up →

1. Go to business.google.com

2. Click "Manage now" and sign in with your Google account

3. Enter business name: "GlobalTechAndTrade"

4. Select category: "Software Company" or "Import Export Company"

5. Add your India office address first, then create a second listing for Zambia

6. Verify via postcard, phone, or email (Google will guide you)

7. Add photos, business hours, services, and description
