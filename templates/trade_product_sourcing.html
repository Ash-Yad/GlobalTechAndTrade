Our Process

How We Source Products

A systematic approach to finding the right suppliers and ensuring quality.

1

Requirement Analysis

We understand your exact specifications, quality standards, quantity requirements, and budget constraints.

2

Supplier Identification

We identify potential suppliers from our verified network and conduct initial screening based on your criteria.

3

Supplier Verification

Factory audits, certification checks, production capacity assessment, and financial stability verification.

4

Sample & Negotiation

We arrange samples for your approval and negotiate the best prices, payment terms, and delivery schedules.

5

Quality Inspection

Pre-shipment inspection, in-process quality checks, and final product verification before dispatch.

6

Order Management

Complete order tracking, production monitoring, and coordination until goods are shipped.
