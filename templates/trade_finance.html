How It Works

Letter of Credit Process

Understanding the LC process helps you trade with confidence.

1

Contract Agreement

Buyer and seller agree on terms including LC as payment method.

2

LC Issuance

Buyer's bank issues LC to seller's bank with payment terms.

3

Shipment & Documents

Seller ships goods and submits compliant documents to bank.

4

Payment Release

Bank verifies documents and releases payment to seller.
